Friends Through the Years

Friendships have played a significant role in my life, shaping my experiences and providing endless support and joy. Here’s a look at the cherished friends I’ve made throughout different stages of my education:


Elementary School Friends



My journey began at Sekolah Kebangsaan Sri Jerai, where I met some of my first and dearest friends. These friendships were built on shared childhood adventures, learning experiences, and the simple joys of growing up together. Though we’ve taken different paths, the memories we created remain a treasured part of my life.



High School Friends



At Maktab Mahmud Merbok, I found another circle of wonderful friends. High school was a time of discovery and growth, and my friends were there every step of the way. From studying for exams to participating in school events, we supported each other through the ups and downs of teenage life. These friendships taught me the value of loyalty and camaraderie.



University Friends

Currently, I’m studying at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM), Kedah Branch, and my university friends have become an essential part of my life. Each phase of my university journey has introduced me to incredible people:



  • Roommate (Semester 1): My first roommate at university was a wonderful companion who made the transition to university life much smoother. We shared many late-night study sessions and heartfelt conversations that helped us bond quickly.








  • Roommate (Semester 2 until now): My current roommate has been with me since the second semester. We’ve grown incredibly close, supporting each other academically and personally. Our shared experiences and mutual understanding have strengthened our friendship.





  • Classmates: My classmates are a diverse group of individuals who bring different perspectives and strengths to our academic environment. Together, we navigate the challenges of our coursework and celebrate each other’s achievements. Their friendship and collaboration make my university experience enriching and fulfilling.








  • My Circle: This special group of friends adds a vibrant and joyful touch to my university life. They always make my life colorful with lots of laughter and fun moments. Whether we’re studying, hanging out, or just chatting, their presence brings immense happiness and energy into my days.

Lifelong Connections

Each of these friendships has left an indelible mark on my heart. From my earliest days in school to my current university life, my friends have been my confidants, cheerleaders, and partners in adventure. I am grateful for each one of them and look forward to creating many more memories together.
