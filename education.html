Early Beginnings



My educational journey began at Tadika Al-Irfan, where I spent my formative years from the age of 4 to 6. These early experiences laid the foundation for my love of learning and curiosity about the world.

Primary School Years



From there, I moved on to Sekolah Kebangsaan Sri Jerai, where I studied from the age of 7 to 12. These years were filled with discovery and growth, as I developed a strong academic foundation and a passion for various subjects.

Secondary School Journey



At the age of 13, I embarked on the next chapter of my education at Maktab Mahmud Merbok. For 5 years, from 13 to 17, I honed my knowledge and skills in a rigorous and supportive environment, preparing myself for future academic challenges.

Current Pursuits



Currently, I am continuing my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM), Kedah Branch, where I am pursuing a Diploma in Library Informatics. This program is a perfect fit for my interests and career aspirations, as it combines my love for information, organization, and helping others access knowledge.
